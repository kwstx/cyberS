# Decentralized Agentic Risk Intelligence Fabric (DARIP)

Decentralized Agentic Risk Intelligence Fabric (DARIP) is a next-generation, AI-driven Third-Party Risk Management and Supply Chain Cybersecurity platform that fundamentally transforms how organizations identify, predict, and mitigate cyber risks across their entire multi-tier vendor ecosystems.

Unlike existing tools that rely on periodic questionnaires, static ratings, and manual workflows, this platform combines:
- Autonomous graph-based supply chain discovery
- Real-time multi-signal data fusion
- Predictive AI modeling
- Multi-agent orchestration

This delivers continuous, high-fidelity visibility and automated remediation at enterprise scale.

Key features and goals:
- Operationalizing SBOMs (Software Bill of Materials)
- Enabling deep fourth- and nth-party mapping
- Incorporating future-proof capabilities for AI model risks
- Integrating post-quantum cryptography
- Reducing manual effort by up to 90%
- Dramatically improving risk prediction accuracy
- Turning reactive compliance into proactive, business-aligned resilience

In essence, DARIP is an intelligent, self-improving risk intelligence system that makes supply chain cybersecurity not just manageable, but a strategic advantage.
